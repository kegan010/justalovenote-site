Shipping & delivery

Every order is hand-assembled and mailed from Orange, California.

How much is shipping?
Shipping is a flat $4.00 per order within the United States, and it's free on orders over $25. You'll see the exact total at checkout before you pay.
How long until my order arrives?
Because each card is handmade, please allow 1–3 business days for me to make and pack your order. Once it ships, U.S. delivery typically takes 3–7 business days. So most orders arrive within about a week to a week and a half.
Do you ship internationally?
Right now the website ships within the United States. If you're outside the U.S. and would love a card, send me a note — I can sometimes arrange international shipping, and any customs or import fees would be the buyer's responsibility.
Will my card get bent in the mail?
Cards are packed carefully with backing to keep them flat and protected. If anything arrives damaged, message me with a photo and I'll make it right.

Orders & payment

Checkout is quick and secure.

What payment methods do you accept?
Checkout is powered by Stripe, so you can pay with all major credit and debit cards, plus options like Apple Pay and other wallets shown at checkout. Your card details go straight to Stripe — they're never stored on this site.
Is my payment information safe?
Yes. Payments are processed by Stripe over a secure, encrypted connection, and this site never sees or stores your full card number.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Since cards are made quickly after you order, please request any changes or a cancellation within 1 hour of purchasing. Reach out as soon as you can and I'll do my best to help before it's made.
Can I include a note or have it sent to the recipient?
Absolutely — just message me after ordering with any special instructions. Cards come blank inside so you can write your own message, or I can help with a custom touch.

Returns & exchanges

I want you to love your cards.

What's your return policy?
If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, please contact me within 14 days of delivery with a photo and I'll send a replacement or refund. Because cards are handmade and personal, I'm not able to accept returns on used items, but I'll always work with you to make things right.
Are custom orders returnable?
Custom and personalized cards are made just for you, so they can't be returned or exchanged — but I'll share a proof when possible so you can approve the design before it's finished.

Digital downloads

Instant printables and SVG cut files.

How do I get my download?
As soon as your payment is confirmed, your download link appears right on the thank-you page. Just click to save the file to your device. (Tip: digital orders skip shipping and the shipping address entirely.)
What will I receive?
Printable cards come as a high-resolution PDF you can print at home or at a print shop. SVG designs come as a file ready for Cricut and other cutting machines. Each listing tells you the format.
Can I get a refund on a digital item?
Because digital files are delivered instantly and can't be returned, all digital sales are final. If you have any trouble downloading or opening a file, message me and I'll help right away.
What can I use the files for?
Downloads are for personal use — print as many as you like for yourself, gifts, and your own events. Please don't resell or share the files. Interested in commercial use? Just ask.

Custom orders & wholesale

Let's make something special.

Can you make a custom card?
Yes — it's my favorite thing! Inside jokes, hobbies, names, dates, colors… tell me about the moment on the custom order form and I'll follow up with ideas, pricing, and a timeline.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
I do. Whether you're stocking a shop or need cards for an event, reach out with what you're looking for and I'll put together bulk pricing.
How long do custom orders take?
It depends on the design and the season, but I'll always give you a clear timeline before we start. I usually reply to custom requests within 1–2 days.

About the cards

A little about what makes them special.

Are the cards really handmade?
Yes — every card is designed and hand-cut by me (hi, I'm Lauren!) in Orange, California. No two are mass-produced; small, lovely variations are part of the handmade charm.
What size are the cards, and do they come with an envelope?
Cards are a standard greeting-card size and arrive blank inside with a coordinating envelope, ready for your message.
Where can I see new designs?
Follow along on Instagram at @justalovenote_ for new cards and seasonal designs, or join the love-letter list on the home page.

Still have a question? 💌

I'm always happy to help — reach out and I'll get back to you within a day or two.
